Responding to Coronavirus

College campuses can help increase hospital capacity

We analyzed the capacity of colleges across the United States to house hospital staff and patients without coronavirus, if hospitals reach capacity.

At Tufts University's MGGG Redistricting Lab, we used our expertise in geospatial analysis to connect hospitals to nearby colleges. We input the latest available data on college dorms and hospital beds.
