Coronavirus Pandemic Response

College campuses can provide much-needed support for hospitals in distress

As the growing number of coronavirus cases continues to deplete limited hospital resources, college campuses that have sent their students home are in a unique position to provide additional beds and facilities to hospitals.

At Tufts University's MGGG Redistricting Lab, we used our expertise in geospatial analysis to connect hospitals to nearby college campuses. We analyzed the ability of colleges across the United States to house hospital staff and patients when hospitals reach capacity.
